# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

## Overview

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics. This course explores the intersection of artificial intelligence and physical systems, focusing specifically on humanoid robots and their applications.

## What You'll Learn

In this textbook, we'll cover:

- Fundamentals of physical AI and embodied intelligence
- Humanoid robot design and mechanics
- AI algorithms optimized for physical interaction
- Sensor integration and processing
- Motion planning and control
- Human-robot interaction
- Applications of humanoid robotics in various fields
- Future directions and emerging technologies

## Course Structure

This textbook is organized into progressive modules that build upon each other to provide a comprehensive understanding of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. Each module combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications and implementation examples.

## Prerequisites

To get the most out of this textbook, you should have:

- Basic understanding of programming concepts
- Familiarity with mathematics (linear algebra, calculus)
- Introduction to AI and machine learning concepts
- Interest in robotics and physical systems

In the following chapters, we'll dive deeper into each of these areas, providing both theoretical foundations and practical implementation examples.
